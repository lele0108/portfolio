Hi, I'm Jimmy. I create beautiful and minimalistic designed tailored toward ease of use. I believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. I've fluent in HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript and Java. My tools of trade are Coda, Adobe PhotoShop, Sublime Text, and Chrome. I'm currently working at VortexUnit.

My favorite video games are Fez, Super Meat Boy, and Bastion. My favorite artists are Helen Jane Long, Hans Zimmer, and Roberto Cacciapaglia. The person that inspires me the most is currenlty Steve Jobs. My current phone is the iPhone 4S and my main computer is a Macbook Pro. I currently reside in Cupertino, California.
